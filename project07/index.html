My Story

I first started skiing when I was around 5 years old at Jiminy Peak. I would go with my aunts, uncles, and cousins—some of my favorite childhood memories were made there.

As I grew older I started snowboarding to add a new side of the adventure. Now I switch between skiing and snowboarding depending on conditions.

Why you should try it

  • Adventure & freedom: every run is a path through beautiful scenery.
  • Health & fitness: a fun way to build endurance and stay active.
  • Community: the mountain brings people together (chairlift talks are the best).
